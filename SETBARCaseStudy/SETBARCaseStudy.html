SETBAR Case Study

Discover SETBAR, a US brand focused on providing healthy and organic nutrition bars, and how they amplified the unboxing experience with high quality display box packaging in collaboration with PackMojo.

SETBAR

SETBAR is an all organic, vegan, gluten free and non-gmo nutrition bar that contains no dairy, soy or refined sugars.

To package its set of 12 nutrition bars, SETBAR used custom display boxes, which are perfect for retail display. These display boxes are pre-glued and can be flattened, which makes it optimal for shipping and storage. What also makes these boxes unique is that they're collapsible, meaning you simply need to 'pop' the box into place for the box to take shape. The result? Ample time saved during the packing process, and no additional glue or tape needed!

I never thought I would find a packaging supplier that was genuinely easy to work and collaborate with until I found PackMojo. Since their website is presentable and easy to navigate, contrary to many other suppliers, it was a huge surprise that their pricing was reasonable for a small business like mine.

Cru Mahoney, Co-Owner of SETBAR
