Six Realms is a Canadian brand established in 2019 with a commitment to creating unique and sustainable fashion accessories. Their products, which include bags and sunglasses, are crafted in Canada and Spain using vegan materials.

PackMojo worked with Six Realms to produce custom magnetic rigid boxes for their line of luxury bags. These luxury packaging boxes have an elegant magnetic lid closure, are made from beige textured paper, and are topped off with a rose gold foil stamped logo. What's more is that this magnetic rigid box is collapsible, making it easy to store!

It was a great experience to work with PackMojo! We had some specific requests on the design of our packaging and they were always willing to provide us with solutions to accommodate. The boxes turned out to be excellent and surpassed our expectations. We definitely recommend them to anyone who is looking for high-quality packaging and a worry-free experience!

- Derrick Liu, Creative Director of Six Realms
