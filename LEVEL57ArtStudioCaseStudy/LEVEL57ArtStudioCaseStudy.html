LEVEL57 Art Studio Case Study

Discover LEVEL57, a US art studio that curates unique collections of art from artists around the world, and how they enhanced the customer experience with shoulder and neck rigid boxes in collaboration with PackMojo.

LEVEL57 Art Studio

LEVEL57 Art Studio provides art that meets the needs of the design and furnishings industry. They partner with Designers, Retailers and Art Consultants to find and create an array of art that will fit the scope of their designs. They specialize in sourcing and developing art for residential, hospitality, corporate and healthcare projects.

LEVEL57 Art Studio wanted a unique way to store and present its art swatches to customers. The shoulder and neck rigid box was optimally sized to fit a set of art swatches inside, allowing anyone to easily flip through each swatch (filing cabinet style) to view and find what they're looking for. This premium shoulder and neck rigid box was custom printed with a bold logo on the lid, along with a patterned design on the neck of the box. This box design is minimal and gives off a feeling of brand luxury.

PackMojo was a pleasure to work with! Incredible customer service, great communication, paid attention to details and quick to respond!

- LEVEL57 Art Studio
